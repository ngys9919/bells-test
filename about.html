About SCTP:

SkillsFuture Career Transition Programme (SCTP) is a train-and-place programme to support mid-career individuals in acquiring industry-relevant skills to improve employability and pivot to new sectors or job roles.
SCTP trainees will attend training courses in addition to Bells Institute of Higher Learning's career coaching services and employment facilitation.
Supports individuals in acquiring industry-relevant skills to improve employability and pivot to new sectors or job roles.
Open to all Singaporeans and Singapore Permanent Residents who are looking for training opportunities to transit to a new sector or job role.
Prepares individuals who are serious in embarking their careers in high- growth IT careers.

About BELLSTECH:

BELLSTECH is a business unit of Bells Institute of Higher Learning, specializing in technology courses, especially the SkillsFuture Career Transition Programmes, namely in the tech areas of cyber-security, data-science, full-stack and block-chain.
